## Name
### Japanese: Jun Kōki (純光希)
- **"Jun"** (純) is Japanese for "Naïve".
- **"Kōki"** is composed of the characters kō (光) meaning "light" and ki (希) meaning "hope".
### Mikkī (ミッキー)
- Derived from the name of the character's creator.
### English: Chún Guāng-xī
- Mandarin Chinese reading of the Japanese name.
### Mickey
- English reading of the Japanese name.
### Chinese: Chún Guāng-xī (純光希)
- Ditto of the English reading.
### Miqi (米奇)
- Mandarin Chinese "translation" of Mikkī. There is no real meaning to this name.
